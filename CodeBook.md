https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip 

Each column name is genereated by the following information


1: Time or Frequency
2: Body or Gravity
3: Acceleration of Gyroscopic
4: Jerk, Magnitude or Jerk Magnitude
5: Mean or Std Dev
6: X, Y or Z
7: Activity names

For example tBodyACCmeanX_Laying

means we have time, body,accelaration,mean,X while laying.

there is a different row for each subject
